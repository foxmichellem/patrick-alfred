Overview

I joined Disney in 2019 to help launch Disney+; the following 7 years, I managed a team of talented animators, designers, and prototypers with the explicit goal of making Disney+ the most loved streaming app. I was partnered with studios, marketing teams, and engineering teams to make sure the best stories in the world were digitally housed in a magical and frictionless experience equalling a Disney Parks experience.

Outcome

10 million signups at launch. We've since grown the service to over 130 million subscribers with localized content in over 60 countries. Disney+ has steadfastly become the central aggregate for all Disney's properties (Hulu, ESPN, ABC, Fox, Broadcast, etc.) and, in 2027, will be the official NFL Super Bowl host.
